About the Author
Jeff Kinney

He's one of the world's most successful children's authors - selling 200 million copies of his Diary of a Wimpy Kid series and meeting three US presidents along the way. Diary of a Wimpy Kid author Jeff Kinney didn’t grow up wanting to be a children’s author. His dream was to become a newspaper cartoonist, but he wasn’t able to get his comic strips syndicated.

About the Publisher
Harry N. Abrams, Inc.

In 2006, Jeff signed a multi-book deal with publisher Harry N. Abrams, Inc. to turn Diary of a Wimpy Kid into a print series. The first Diary of a Wimpy Kid book was published in 2007 and became an instant bestseller. Just a year later, more than 100,000 copies were in print in the United States alone.
